Committees

Technical Committee

The Technical Committee is responsible for organize and offer skill development workshops to members of BMES. Recently, these workshops have covered topics including arduino programming, use of power tools, effective technical presentation skills, and design with CAD software. In addition, the Technical Committee oversees the development of student projects for Engineering Open House.

Social Committee

The Social Committee members are each responsible for a specific category of events within the Social Committee. For instance, there will be a committee member responsible for the journal discussions, intramural teams, and the mentoring program. The Social Committee also organizes the highly popular seasonal picnics!

Outreach Committee

The Outreach Committee is responsible for organizing and executing service events within the university and the community. Committee members will be responsible for planning their own service events, organizing review sessions for class exams, helping lead prospective students on tours, and raising funds for Relay For Life.

Treasury Committee

The Treasury Committee is responsible for planning and executing fundraising events that help support our community as well as our BMES chapter. Committee members will be responsible for managing their own events, helping find funding opportunities for our chapter, and planning the annual End-of-the-Year Banquet.
