Contact Us

To join BMES, simply come to our next General Meeting and talk to one of the members on our Executive Board. These meetings are held monthly, with dates and locations posted on our calendar. Members of BMES pay $15 dues annually, in order to offset the cost of food provided at our events, and support the development of new activities.

Companies considering sponsoring BMES should take a look through our brochure (below), and may contact the Vice President or Industrial Relations Chair for more information.


Other forms of keeping in touch with our organization are enumerated below. Don't be afraid to reach out!
