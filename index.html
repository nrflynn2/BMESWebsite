Biomedical Engineering Society
UIUC Student Chapter

The Biomedical Engineering Society (BMES) is the professional society for biomedical engineering and bioengineering. Interdisciplinary collaboration is intrinsic within the field of bioengineering, and this extends to our student chapter - we promote technological diversity and welcome students from all divisions of campus. Our chapter offers: social events between students, professors, and potential employers; project experience through participation in EOH and the national BMES design competition; and professional development workshops and career events. Otherwise, if you would just like to chat then swing by our office located at DCL 3101!
